Research

I am currently a research intern studying the transfer learning of Graph Neural Networks (under the supervision of Juan Ye and Peter Mann).

Our aim is to better understand how Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) can be used to capture the general structure of networks, and how this can be used to train a GNN for use on unseen networks. In particular, we are looking at training a GNN on a small network, and using this to make predictions about a structurally similar, large network. This could be used, for example, to predict the spread of a large epidemic based on early infections.
